Autoplay not working on one device on Windows en

So, for some reason somewhere around this week, Dropbox stopped importing pictures from my Moto G. Looking at the autoplay settings everything was setup correctly. I tried reseting everything with no avail, and I tried restarting the Shell Hardware Detection service without results.

So then I went to the Device Manager, I went to Portable Devices, and uninstalled my cellphone, listed as XT1032, for some reason.

Then I unplugged the phone, connected it again, let Windows detect it, and voilá! Autoplay is working again, and I got Dropbox to import my stuff! Yay!

So, the moral of the story is: if everything fails, reinstall.
